Know Us

ENHANCE THE CHARACTER OF YOUR BRAND

Our Company was established in May 2000, which was experienced manufacturer and exporter of a most excellent range of Mannequins and Dummies. The company is considered a leading name in the industry, engaged in creating highest quality mannequins and dummies and also takes pride in serving the customers efficiently. We see it as our duty to be environmentally conscious, to conserve natural resources and thus our profitability and competitiveness in the long term. We Abstract Mannequins Based in Delhi(India) and for generations has designed and produced mannequins for many of the world's leading retailers. A fashion leader and innovator, we regularly work on the most exciting visual merchandising projects in retail.
