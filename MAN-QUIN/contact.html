Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Do you offer expedited shipping?

Most of the time we cannot offer expedited shipping. Additionally, shipping of large items like mannequins via overnight or three - day air is insanely expensive. Same day and next day processing of orders with a $40 rush fee is available on case by case basis.

How quickly can I get my order?

Normally we ship 2-3 business days after you place your order. You will receive the tracking number when your order ships.
If you need something by a specific date, call/email us in advance so we can confirm whether or not it will arrive on time.

Mannequins are not that heavy, why is shipping so expensive?

The reason shipping mannequins is so expensive is that the boxes are bulky - they almost hit the maximum allowed shipping size for regular parcel delivery.
As a result, the shipping carriers (FEDEX and UPS) charge us based on the size of the box or the weight, whichever results in the higher shipping charge..
